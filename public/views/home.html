We Love Computing.

Temple ACM is dedicated to expanding the academic and professional knowledge of computing across campus. We are also dedicated to helping our students and faculty to cooperate and grow together. Our entire organization is fueled by its collective passion for the boundless possibilities of emerging technology and the creativity it inspires. The world of computing invokes a different perspective on the world and sparks the imagination of its students. We are proud to be evangelists of our craft, and hope to create some incredible technologists.

We Are a Community.

Temple ACM welcomes people of all majors and creeds to meetings to encourages exploration and discussion of a wide variety of computing-related ideas. We also contend to connect students not only with a vast network of companies, but also to the ever-growing community of their peers. As students, we have a unique opportunity to explore that is rare later in life, and we believe that attending events like hack-a-thons and conferences as a group encourages growth of programming know-how and camraderie.

We Strive To Innovate.

Temple ACM is dedicated to the idea that innovation is the key driver of technology. That technology is a key piece of our everyday lives is already evident, but pushing technology forward is all about figuring out improvements to the human experience and pursuing them. We try to emphasize the value of brainstorming at meetings and have a great culture around communal support for ideas within the group, but understand that as the next generation of developers we must be the harbringers of the next generation of ideas - for better of for worse.
